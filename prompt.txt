```AI Engineering Assessment
Overview
Build a working demo that answers a business question about SEC financial filings using a

Timebox: ~4 hours
The Task
Build a retrieval index over the provided corpus and a prompt template that injects retrieved

Document your assumptions and the decisions you make along the way.
Dataset
The corpus is a collection of SEC EDGAR filings — annual reports (10-K) and quarterly

edgar_corpus.zip
The zip contains .txt filings and a manifest.json.

During the demo, we will enter a business question into your system's input field. Examples

- "What are the primary risk factors facing Apple, Tesla, and JPMorgan, and how do

- "How has NVIDIA's revenue and growth outlook changed over the last two years?"
- "What regulatory risks do the major pharmaceutical companies face, and how are
they addressing them?"
Deliverables
Submit a repo or notebook containing:
- A README with setup and run instructions
- Your indexing and retrieval code
- A log of your prompt iterations (what changed, why)
- Your final prompt template
- An example request ready to execute
- Notes on how you evaluated quality
Demo Format
We'll clone your repo, set up the environment using your instructions, and run a business

Questions
